I'm the **Executive Director** of the [Open Commercial Media Ecosystem (OCME)](https://ocmeco.org), a 501(c)(6) member-governed nonprofit rebuilding the creator economy on top of cryptographically secure, tamper-evident identity.

I went full-time on August 20, 2025. OCME is the operational expression of my first mission — bringing self-sovereign identity (SSI) technology to **100 million people by 2034** — and the engine of my second — restoring economic agency to creators and professionals through alternatives to gatekeeper-controlled platforms.

## Why OCME exists

The platforms that distribute creative work today own the audience, the data, the discovery surface, and the rules. Creators rent everything. When the algorithm shifts or the contract changes, value built over years can evaporate in a quarter — see [The First Warning Shot](/ideas/the-first-warning-shot/).

OCME flips the model. Creators, curators, and industry members are members of the ecosystem. Identity, content, plays, and payments are all anchored to decentralized identifiers (DIDs) the creators control. The infrastructure is open-by-default; the governance is industry-led, not platform-led.
